Montana is an acrylic painter based in Montreal, QC. Her practice consists of work on canvas, in sketchbooks, and, more recently, on paper for her 2022 100 Day Project. In her style of unblended mark-making, she explores how to create a piece with a tension between loose marks and a tight composition.

The best ways to stay up to date on her work are to subscribe to her newsletter, or follow her on Instagram (@montanafowler_cs_art).
